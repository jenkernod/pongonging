Shadow of the Colossus: The Importance of Gaius and Size Perspective

This purpose of this project is to share the first strong, memorable experience I had when playing a video game. Shadow of The Colossus, orginally a PS2 game from 2005 that has been remastered for both the PS3 and PS4 due to it's popularity. You start the game as this young man thrown into a very open world with the hopes of reviving a girl. In order to revive the girl, he must fight and kill 16 colossi across this strange fantasy world. The most memorable of the colossi is the third one you fight, The Knight, AKA Gaius. You must swim onto a bridge ascending to this hovering platform over a lake under dark clouds. When you reach the top of the platform, the large pile of rocks in the center start standing up and form Gaius. I recommend playing the game as it contains such beautiful imagery and landscaping along with the intense designs of each of the foes. Thank You!
